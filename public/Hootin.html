You've made it!

Welcome to the Hootin music page. There isn't a heck of a lot going on here, I just wanted to give you a place to come and get a hold of my album! I want to give a huge thanks to Brin Porter who worked his tuccas off in the studio with me. Without him this would be a much scratchier unpolished mess and probably would never have happened.

When you are ready head over to the Get The Music!
